EMEA Opportunity Analysis

1. EMEA Win / Loss Split

Shows the overall split between Closed Won and Closed Lost across all opportunities in the period.

2. Win Rate by Fiscal Quarter

Win rate percentage for each fiscal quarter. Higher bar = more deals won relative to total.

3. Win Rate by Month — Seasonality

Win rate for each calendar month. Shows which months tend to close more successfully.

4. Opportunity Volume by Month

Total number of opportunities (won + lost) per month. Colour shows win rate — darker = higher win rate.

5. Win Rate by Opportunity Type

Win rate split by deal type (new customer, existing customer etc.).

6. Win Rate by Sales Motion

Win rate for each sales motion: inbound, outbound, outbound field, partnerships.

7. Median Days to Close by Sales Motion

The median number of days from opportunity creation to close, per sales motion. Colour shows win rate.

8. MRR Distribution — Closed Won

Distribution of MRR values across all won deals. Shows the typical deal size range.

9. Win Rate by Country

Win rate per country. Colour shows average MRR — darker = higher average deal size.

10. Sales Motion Mix by Country

Number of opportunities per sales motion for each country. Shows how each market is covered.

11. Seasonality by Country

Monthly win rate per country. Use the legend to show/hide countries.

12. Win Rate by Time to Close & Country

Win rate split by how long the deal took to close per country. Faster deals generally win more.

13. Top 20 Cities by Wins (All Motions)

Cities with the highest number of Closed Won deals. Colour shows win rate — darker = higher win rate.

13b. Top 20 Cities — Field Sales Only

Same as above but filtered to outbound field deals only.

14. Hamburg vs Paris

Win rate comparison between Hamburg and Paris, split by sales motion.

15. Win Rate by Business Type

Win rate per business type (min 5 deals). Colour shows average MRR.

16. Business Type x Country Heatmap

Win rate for each combination of business type and country. Green = higher win rate, white = no data.

17. Win Rate by Merchant Profile

Win rate split by merchant profile (low, medium, high, key).

18. Win Rate vs Each Competitor

Win rate when a specific competitor was identified as primary. Colour shows how many times that competitor was faced.

19. Systems Most Often Displaced

The previous systems most commonly replaced in Closed Won deals.

20. Median Days to Close by Competitor

How long deals take when competing against each competitor. Longer = harder or more complex deals.

21. Top Loss Reasons

The most common reasons for losing deals. Percentage shown on each bar.

22. Loss Reasons by Country

Loss reason breakdown per country. Darker red = higher count.

23. Lead Source Quality

Each bubble is a lead source. X = volume, Y = win rate, size and colour = avg MRR. Best sources are top-right with large bubbles.

24. Avg MRR — Talk to Expert vs Watch a Demo

Average MRR of won deals from each of these two lead sources.

25. Merchant Profile — Talk to Expert vs Demo

Profile mix of opportunities from Talk to an Expert vs Watch a Demo.

26. Rep Efficiency Matrix — Field Reps

Each bubble is a field rep. X = number of deals, Y = win rate, bubble size = avg deal size, colour = country. Top-right = high volume and high win rate.

27. Rep Efficiency Matrix — by Business Type

Field rep performance split by business type. Helps identify which reps win in which segments.

28. Win Rate by Time to Close & Team

Win rate split by deal speed for each team/role. Shows which teams close faster and whether speed affects their win rate.

29. Win Rate by Time to Close & Merchant Profile

Win rate split by deal speed for each merchant profile.

30. Reps with Longest Sales Cycles

Reps with the highest median days to close (min 20 opportunities).

31. Win Rate by Owner Role

Win rate per role type across the team.

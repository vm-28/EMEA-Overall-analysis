Chapter 1 — Overall EMEA Performance

High-level summary of win/loss, deal size, seasonality and sales motion across all markets.

Win rate and total opportunity count per fiscal quarter. The percentage shows how many deals we won out of all deals that quarter.

Win rate for each calendar month. Shows which months close most successfully.

4. Opportunity Volume and Win Rate by Month

Bars show total opportunities per month. The red line shows win rate. A tall bar with a low line means lots of pipeline but poor conversion that month.

5. Win Rate by Opportunity Type

Win rate per deal type. Number in brackets shows total opportunities for that type.

6. Win Rate by Sales Motion

Win rate per sales motion. Number in brackets shows total opportunities. Blank sales motion rows have been excluded.

7. Median Days to Close by Sales Motion

The middle value of days from opportunity creation to close, per sales motion. Colour shows win rate — darker means higher win rate.

8. MRR Distribution — Closed Won

Distribution of MRR values across won deals. The spike on the left shows most deals are small. The tail to the right shows a smaller number of larger deals.

Chapter 3 — Business Analysis

What types of businesses we win, GTV revenue bands, and merchant profiles.

17. Win Rate by Business Type

Win rate per business type (min 5 deals). Colour = average MRR — darker means bigger deals.

Win rate split by merchant profile: low, medium, high, key. Number in brackets = total opportunities.

Chapter 4 — Competitive Analysis

Who we compete against, where we lose, and how competition affects deal speed.

26. Win Rate vs Each Competitor

Win rate when a specific competitor was identified as the main competitor. Colour = times faced — darker means more common. Blank, other, and unknown entries are excluded.

27. Win Rate by Competitor and Country

Win rate against each competitor broken down by country. Green = we win more, red = we lose more. White = not enough data.

28. Systems Most Often Replaced

This shows the previous POS system or software the customer was using before switching to us. Based on the Current System field in Salesforce. Blank, other, and unknown entries are excluded.

29. Median Days to Close by Competitor

How long deals take when each competitor is involved. Longer bars mean those deals typically take more time to decide.

30. Top Loss Reasons — EMEA

Number of losses per reason per country. Darker red = higher count.

33. Loss Reasons by Business Type — Heatmap

Number of losses per reason for the top 10 business types.

34. Loss Reasons by Business Type — % Breakdown

For each business type, what proportion of losses fall into each reason. Each bar adds up to 100%.

35. Loss Reasons by GTV Band

Loss reasons broken down by GTV revenue band. Shows whether smaller or larger businesses lose for different reasons.
